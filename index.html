Our Mission

The summer before my junior year of high school, I visited Maison Fortuné Orphanage in Hinche, Haiti, where I found overwhelming disparities between the boys’ and girls’ campuses in both educational opportunities and material belongings.

Upon returning home, I started this campaign through a nonprofit organization in Chesapeake, Virginia to support the girls of Maison Fortuné. My campaign focuses on raising enough money to hire local workers in Hinche to construct a guest house on the girls’ campus (similar to an existing one on the boys’); the success of such a project would increase the girls’ interaction with a wider community, which in turn, would power the effectiveness of the campus by bringing donations of money from visitors that could be used to buy educational tools or other materials. With a regular stream of people and donations, improvements on the girls’ campus would be self-sustaining.
